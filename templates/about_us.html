About Us

DeliSteak first opened its doors in the late 1950's. We started as a small local diner in the heart of Hartford, Connecticut, and have since expanded rapidly. DeliSteak is committed to serving the community fresh, innovative dishes while trying to constantly expand and improve. We are now located across 7 states along the East Coast and wish to expand further. Come to visit us anytime at any of our locations, we promise you will not be disappointed!
